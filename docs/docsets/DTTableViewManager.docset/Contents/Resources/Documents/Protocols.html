Protocols

The following protocols are available globally.

  • DTTableViewManageable

    Adopting this protocol will automatically inject manager property to your object, that lazily instantiates DTTableViewManager object. Target is not required to be UITableViewController, and can be a regular UIViewController with UITableView, or even different object like UICollectionViewCell.
